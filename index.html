Our Story

Presence, not procedure

Sacred Pet Doula was founded on a simple belief: that animals deserve the same dignity, tenderness, and ceremony in their final chapter that we would want for the people we love. We are not a clinic. We are a hand to hold — human and animal alike — through anticipatory grief, active transition, and the long tender work of remembrance.

Our mission is to help you slow down, listen to what your companion is asking for, and honor this passage as the sacred one it is.

Presence

We sit with what is, without rushing toward what's next.

Reverence

Every transition is treated as sacred, never clinical.

Honesty

Gentle truth-telling, so you can make peace, not just decisions.

Continuity

Support doesn't end at goodbye — remembrance is part of the care.
